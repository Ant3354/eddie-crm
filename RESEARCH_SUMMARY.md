### 1. **Advanced Analytics & Reporting** ⭐⭐⭐

- Custom reports
- Export capabilities

**Why**: Industry trend - AI is transforming CRM
- Auto-generate personalized emails
- Predictive analytics (churn, conversion)

- Smart automation

### 3. **Enhanced User Experience** ⭐⭐⭐
**Why**: Better UX = higher adoption and productivity
- Advanced search & filtering
- Bulk operations
- Activity timeline
- Keyboard shortcuts
- Dark mode

### 4. **Real-time Features** ⭐⭐
**Why**: Faster response = better customer service
- Live notifications

### 5. **Enhanced Integrations** ⭐⭐
**Why**: More touchpoints = better relationships

- API webhooks

### 6. **Advanced Campaign Features** ⭐⭐
**Why**: Better campaigns = more conversions
- A/B testing
- Conditional logic
- Visual campaign builder
- Advanced segmentation

### 7. **Contact Intelligence** ⭐⭐
**Why**: Better targeting = higher success rates
- Contact scoring
- Relationship mapping
- Data enrichment
- Interaction history

### 8. **Security & Compliance** ⭐⭐⭐
**Why**: Critical for trust and legal compliance
- 2FA authentication
- GDPR/CCPA tools
- Enhanced audit logging
- Role-based access

### 9. **Performance & Scalability** ⭐
**Why**: Fast = better user experience
- Database optimization

- Performance monitoring

### 10. **UI/UX Enhancements** ⭐
**Why**: Modern design = professional appearance
- Customizable dashboards
